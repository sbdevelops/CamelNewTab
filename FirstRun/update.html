Camel New Tab was updated! (Version 2.4.0)



There are now multiple types of New Tab pages that you can use!

Camel New Tab now lets you take charge of your new tab page by giving multiple options for when you open a new tab. You can change your New Tab page any time by simply opening the Camel New Tab menu at the top right of your screen by clicking the camel icon, and opening the settings menu. If you do not select a new tab page below, you will automatically be set to use the Original Camel New Tab page. There are many options for New Tab pages, including:

  • The Original Camel New Tab
  • A simplified version of Camel New Tab
  • Alexander Hamilton New Tab Page
  • And many more to come!
